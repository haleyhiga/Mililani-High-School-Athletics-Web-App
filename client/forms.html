Athlete Forms

**Please submit completed forms to either Athletic Trainer, Toby Wolff or Jamie Sardinha. Their mailbox is located in the main admin office.

In order to participate in sports at Mililani High School, all student athletes MUST MEET THE FOLLOWING:

  • Submit a current Athletics Sports Physical Form done within the last calendar year
  • Submit Student Participation and Parent/Legal Guardian Consent, Release
  • Enrolled at Mililani High School (live in MHS district or GE on file)
  • All school/class fees and obligations are cleared
  • Meets academic requirements (minimum GPA 2.0 and no 'F's in any required courses for graduation)
