Frequently Asked Questions

What forms are required to participate in sports? expand_more

You need to submit a current Athletics Sports Physical Form (done within the last calendar year), and the Student Participation & Parent/Guardian Consent Release form.

What academic requirements must I meet? expand_more

You must have a GPA of 2.0 or higher and no F's in required courses for graduation.

When are tryouts and practices? expand_more

Dates and times vary by sport. Check the homepage or Athletics Calendar for up-to-date information.

What sports are offered in each season? expand_more

Fall: Air Riflery, Bowling, Cheer, Cross Country, Football, Girls Volleyball, JV Softball, Soft Tennis
Winter: Basketball, Wrestling, Paddling, Soccer, Swimming/Diving, JV Baseball, JV Tennis
Spring: Flag Football, Girls Water Polo, Tennis, Judo, Boys Volleyball, Golf, Track & Field, Varsity Softball/Baseball

Where do I turn in my completed forms? expand_more

Give your forms to Athletic Trainers Toby Wolff or Jamie Sardinha. Their mailbox is in the main admin office.

Can I download the forms online? expand_more

Yes! You can find the downloadable forms on our website under the Forms page.

Where can I view the full athletics schedule? expand_more

You can view all events and game dates on our Mililani Athletics Calendar.

What should I bring to tryouts? expand_more

Each sport varies, but generally bring: water bottle, appropriate athletic wear, cleats/shoes, and any gear listed by the coach.
